Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Delivery Areas

We ship to all UK mainland addresses. Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands, Channel Islands, and international orders may be subject to additional charges and extended delivery times — please contact us before ordering.

```

2. Dispatch Times

Orders placed before 1pm Monday to Friday (excluding UK bank holidays) are typically dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 1pm or on weekends are dispatched the next working day. During busy periods, dispatch may take up to 2 working days.

3. Delivery Times

Standard UK delivery takes 1–5 working days from dispatch. Larger items (espresso machines, grinders) may require a specialist courier and take 2–5 working days. You will receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.

4. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are calculated at the point of sale based on item size, weight, and delivery location. Free delivery may be available on qualifying orders — this will be confirmed during the ordering process.

5. Failed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt is unsuccessful, the courier will leave a card with instructions. Unclaimed parcels will be returned to us after the courier's holding period. Re-delivery charges may apply.

6. Damaged in Transit

All orders are carefully packaged. If your item arrives visibly damaged, please refuse delivery if possible and contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement or refund promptly.

7. Trade & Bulk Orders

For large or trade orders involving multiple machines or bulk accessories, please contact us directly at contact@thefoodcafe.co.uk for a customised delivery quote and timeline.
```
